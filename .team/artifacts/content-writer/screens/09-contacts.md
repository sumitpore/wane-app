## Notes
- Contacts screen is purely functional. No branding, no water motifs.
- If there are no favorites, the section simply doesn't appear. No empty state message for favorites — that would be a nudge.
- The search is immediate (filter-as-you-type), not a separate search screen.
- Tapping a contact shows two simple options: Call or Message. No contact detail view.
