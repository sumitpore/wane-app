---

## Notes
- The dialer is deliberately plain. It is a tool, not a branded experience.
- No water animation in the background. A clean break to make the call, then back to water.
- The back button returns to the water screen, not the home screen.
- Emergency numbers always connect instantly — no interference of any kind.
