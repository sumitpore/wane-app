## Notes
- The home screen is deliberately sparse. No motivational quotes. No tips carousel. No banner ads for premium.
- Streak is stated as fact, not celebration. "Three days of water." — not "3-day streak! Keep going!"
- No session count for today. We don't track frequency as a metric the user sees.
- Returning users see exactly what they left. No re-engagement popups.
- The Start button is the largest element. One tap to water.
