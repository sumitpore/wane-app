# Screen 01 — Welcome

**Flow:** Onboarding (first launch only)
**Purpose:** First impression IS the product. Maximum restraint.

---

## Content

### Visual
Full-screen water animation. No splash screen. No logo. No app name. Just water.

### Text Elements

| Element | Copy |
|---------|------|
| **Bottom prompt** | Tap to begin. |

That's it. One line of text on the entire screen.

---

## Microcopy

| Scenario | Copy |
|----------|------|
| **If animation is loading (>2s)** | _(No text. Show a still water-colored gradient until animation loads.)_ |

---

## Accessibility

| Element | Screen Reader Text |
|---------|-------------------|
| **Water animation** | "Water animation filling the screen." |
| **Tap to begin prompt** | "Tap anywhere to begin setup." |
| **Full screen** | "Welcome to Wane. A water animation is playing. Tap anywhere to continue." |

---

## Notes
- No app name displayed. The water is the introduction.
- No "Welcome to Wane!" — the animation speaks for itself.
- The prompt "Tap to begin." uses a period, not an exclamation mark. Calm punctuation.
- Text appears after a 1.5s delay, giving the water animation time to land.
- Text fades in at low opacity — present but not demanding.
