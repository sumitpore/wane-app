## Notes
- Settings are grouped by purpose, not alphabetically.
- Descriptions are one line maximum. They explain what the setting does, not why you should use it.
- "Your data stays on your phone. Always." — the privacy statement is a point of pride, placed in Settings where trust-conscious users look.
- The share text is casual and intriguing: "My phone becomes water sometimes." Not a sales pitch.
- Emergency contacts and water themes are v2 features -- not shown in v1 settings.
