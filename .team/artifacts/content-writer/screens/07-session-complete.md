# Screen 07 — Session Complete

**Flow:** Active session → completion
**Purpose:** Gentle re-entry. Understated, not celebratory.

---

## Content

### Animation
Water drains to the bottom of the screen and disappears. The home screen fades in behind it. A single brief haptic pulse.

### Text Elements

| Element | Copy |
|---------|------|
| **Fade-in message** | Welcome back. |
| **Duration on screen** | 2 seconds, then fades out |

### Action Buttons (appear after message fades)

---

## Notes
- "Welcome back." — two words and a period. The restraint is the message.
- No confetti. No stats. No "You were away for 1 hour and 23 minutes." No "Great job!"
- The streak is a quiet fact, not a trophy. "Five days of water." stated the same way you'd say "Five days of rain."
- "Done" is the primary button. Most people are done. "Start another" is secondary for those who want it.
- The emotional register is a gentle return, like surfacing from underwater.
