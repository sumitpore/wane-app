## Notes
- "Get this water" instead of "Buy" or "Purchase." Softer, and consistent with the app's language.
- "Still Water" as the default theme name — evocative and descriptive without being generic ("Default").
- No urgency tactics. No "Sale ends in..." or scarcity pressure. The seasonal themes are simply present during their season.
- Theme names are drawn from natural water phenomena. No clever puns or brand-speak.
- The purchase flow is minimal. Tap theme → preview → "Get this water" → done.
- After purchase, the theme is applied immediately. No "Do you want to apply this theme now?" dialog.
